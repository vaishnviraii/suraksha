FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • 1. What is the number for the child helpline in India?

    Answer: The national child helpline number in India is 1098.

  • 2. What services does the child helpline provide?

    Answer: The child helpline provides services such as emergency assistance, rescue operations, counseling, medical aid, shelter, rehabilitation, and referrals to other child protection services.

  • 3. Is the child helpline available 24/7?

    Answer: Yes, the child helpline operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  • 4. Can I remain anonymous when calling the child helpline?

    Answer: Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when calling the child helpline. Your privacy and confidentiality will be respected.

  • 5. What should I do if I suspect a child is being abused?

    Answer: If you suspect a child is being abused, immediately contact the child helpline at 1098. Provide as much information as possible to help them take prompt action.

  • 6. How can the child helpline assist in cases of child labor?

    Answer: The child helpline can assist in cases of child labor by rescuing the child, providing shelter and rehabilitation, and coordinating with authorities to take legal action against those responsible.

  • 7. What support is available for street children?

    Answer: Street children can receive emergency assistance, shelter, medical care, counseling, education, and rehabilitation services through the child helpline.

  • 8. Can the child helpline help with issues related to missing children?

    Answer: Yes, the child helpline can assist with issues related to missing children by coordinating with law enforcement agencies, providing support to the families, and helping in the search and rescue operations.

  • 9. What should I do if a child needs medical attention but has no access to healthcare?

    Answer: If a child needs medical attention and has no access to healthcare, contact the child helpline at 1098. They can arrange for medical assistance and ensure the child receives necessary care.

  • 10. How can the child helpline assist with educational support for underprivileged children?

    Answer: The child helpline can connect underprivileged children with educational programs, provide information on scholarships, and help enroll them in schools or educational institutions.

  • 11. What type of counseling is available through the child helpline?

    Answer: The child helpline offers emotional and psychological counseling to children in distress, helping them cope with trauma, abuse, neglect, and other issues affecting their well-being.

  • 12. Can the child helpline help with adoption processes?

    Answer: The child helpline can provide information and guidance on the legal adoption process, connect prospective parents with authorized adoption agencies, and offer support throughout the process.

  • 13. What should I do if I witness child trafficking?

    Answer: If you witness child trafficking, immediately contact the child helpline at 1098. They can coordinate with law enforcement and other agencies to rescue the child and take necessary legal actions.

  • 14. How can I report bullying or harassment of a child?

    Answer: To report bullying or harassment of a child, contact the child helpline at 1098. Provide details of the incident and the child involved so they can take appropriate action.

  • 15. Are the services of the child helpline free of charge?

    Answer: Yes, the services provided by the child helpline are free of charge.

  • 16. Can the child helpline assist with issues related to child marriage?

    Answer: Yes, the child helpline can assist with issues related to child marriage by intervening to prevent the marriage, providing legal support, and ensuring the safety and well-being of the child.

  • 17. How can I volunteer or support the child helpline?

    Answer: To volunteer or support the child helpline, you can contact them directly to inquire about volunteer opportunities or donate to organizations that support child protection and welfare.

  • 18. What should I do if a child is in immediate danger?

    Answer: If a child is in immediate danger, call the child helpline at 1098 right away. Provide details of the situation and location to enable prompt rescue and intervention.

  • 19. How can I get legal advice for a child in need?

    Answer: The child helpline can connect you with legal experts who can provide advice and assistance on various issues such as abuse, neglect, custody, and other child protection matters.

  • 20. Can the child helpline provide support for children with disabilities?

    Answer: Yes, the child helpline provides support for children with disabilities, including access to medical care, special education, counseling, and other necessary services.
