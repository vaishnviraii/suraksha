The Disaster Management Helpline is designed to provide 24-hour immediate assistance to individuals affected by natural or man-made disasters. The helpline offers emergency response through coordination with relevant authorities such as local police, fire departments, medical services, and relief organizations, ensuring timely support and resources during disasters.

108 is a toll-free 24-hour emergency helpline made available to citizens of Mumbai, Maharashtra, and India to report disasters and request immediate help.

108 Emergency Helpline is integrated with the 112 - Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) for coordinated and quick responses to all types of disasters.

In the future, efforts will be made to streamline services under a single helpline number 112 for all emergency responses, with 108 focusing on disaster-related support, providing detailed information and guidance to citizens during critical situations.

The Disaster Management Helpline will also offer updates on disaster preparedness plans, relief operations, and connect individuals with relevant authorities such as Disaster Management Officers, Emergency Response Teams, and NGOs working to assist victims in affected areas.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS: DISASTER MANAGEMENT HELPLINE

  • 1. What is the number for the disaster management helpline in India?

    Answer: The national disaster management helpline number in India is 1070.

  • 2. What services does the disaster management helpline provide?

    Answer: The disaster management helpline provides services such as emergency assistance, rescue operations, medical aid, and information on evacuation and relief efforts during natural disasters.

  • 3. Is the disaster management helpline available 24/7?

    Answer: Yes, the disaster management helpline operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, especially during emergencies.

  • 4. Can I remain anonymous when calling the disaster management helpline?

    Answer: Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when calling the helpline. The services are designed to respect your privacy while assisting in emergency situations.

  • 5. What should I do if I am trapped in a natural disaster?

    Answer: If you are trapped during a natural disaster, immediately call the disaster management helpline at 1070 for rescue and support. Provide your location and details of your condition for effective assistance.

  • 6. How can the disaster management helpline assist in flood situations?

    Answer: The helpline can provide information about evacuation routes, shelters, and medical aid. It can also coordinate rescue operations with local authorities and provide real-time updates on water levels.

  • 7. What support is available during earthquakes?

    Answer: During earthquakes, the helpline can guide you on safety measures, assist with evacuation, and connect you with emergency services like medical aid and search-and-rescue teams.

  • 8. Can the disaster management helpline help with relief efforts during cyclones?

    Answer: Yes, the helpline provides information on cyclone warnings, evacuation centers, and safety protocols. It also coordinates relief efforts and connects affected individuals with necessary resources.

  • 9. What should I do if I suspect someone is trapped in a disaster zone?

    Answer: If you suspect someone is trapped, call the disaster management helpline and provide their location. The authorities will dispatch rescue teams to assist in their rescue.

  • 10. How can I get legal assistance for disaster-related issues?

    Answer: The disaster management helpline can connect you with legal aid services that provide support in matters like property loss, compensation, and other legal assistance related to disasters.

  • 11. What type of counseling is available through the disaster management helpline?

    Answer: The helpline offers emotional support and counseling services to individuals affected by disasters, helping them cope with trauma, grief, and stress.

  • 12. Can the disaster management helpline assist with temporary shelter?

    Answer: Yes, the helpline can help arrange for temporary shelter and basic necessities for those displaced by natural disasters.

  • 13. What should I do if I am stranded during a flood or storm?

    Answer: If you are stranded, call the disaster management helpline for assistance. Provide your location and any other relevant details so that rescue operations can be initiated.

  • 14. Is there support available for people with disabilities during a disaster?

    Answer: Yes, the helpline ensures that people with disabilities receive the necessary support, including evacuation assistance, medical care, and access to shelters during disasters.

  • 15. Can the disaster management helpline help with relocating to a safer place?

    Answer: Yes, the helpline can assist in arranging relocation to a safer location in the event of imminent danger, such as during floods or cyclones.

  • 16. What should I do if I witness someone in need of emergency medical care during a disaster?

    Answer: If you witness someone needing emergency medical care, contact the disaster management helpline immediately to report the situation and request medical support.

  • 17. Are the services of the disaster management helpline free of charge?

    Answer: Yes, the services provided by the disaster management helpline are free of charge.

  • 18. How can I volunteer or support the disaster management efforts?

    Answer: To volunteer or support the disaster management efforts, you can contact the helpline or local authorities for information on how you can contribute through donations or by joining relief operations.
