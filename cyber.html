The Cyber Helpline Services of Maharashtra are designed to provide 24-hour immediate assistance and support to individuals affected by cybercrimes, including cyber harassment, financial fraud, and identity theft. The helpline connects individuals to the cyber police, forensic experts, and provides information on cyber safety programs and resources across Maharashtra.

1930 is a toll-free 24-hour service available to individuals in Maharashtra seeking help with cyber-related issues, ranging from online scams to data security concerns.

The Cyber Helpline is integrated with the national emergency number 112, part of the Emergency Response Support System (ERSS), to ensure comprehensive support in both emergency and non-emergency cyber situations.

In the future, efforts will focus on a unified system with 112 handling emergency cyber incidents, while 1930 will continue to provide information and non-emergency assistance to citizens.

The Cyber Helpline also offers guidance on cybersecurity programs, connects callers with appropriate authorities for legal support, and provides information on cyber laws. Assistance is available for navigating reporting procedures under laws like the Information Technology Act and other relevant regulations, as per the requirements within Maharashtra.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS: Cybersecurity

  • 1. What is the helpline number for reporting cybersecurity issues in India?

    Answer: The national cybersecurity helpline number in India is 1930.

  • 2. What services does the cybersecurity helpline provide?

    Answer: The cybersecurity helpline assists with reporting online fraud, phishing, identity theft, ransomware attacks, and other cyber threats. It also provides guidance on safeguarding personal data.

  • 3. Is the cybersecurity helpline available 24/7?

    Answer: Yes, the cybersecurity helpline operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to address emergencies and provide assistance.

  • 4. Can I report a cybercrime anonymously?

    Answer: Yes, you can report cybercrimes anonymously to protect your identity and privacy.

  • 5. What should I do if I fall victim to online financial fraud?

    Answer: Immediately call the cybersecurity helpline at 1930 to report the fraud. They can help block the transaction and guide you on further actions.

  • 6. How can the helpline assist with phishing scams?

    Answer: The cybersecurity helpline provides steps to identify phishing emails or messages, report them, and secure your accounts against unauthorized access.

  • 7. What should I do if my social media account is hacked?

    Answer: Contact the cybersecurity helpline for guidance on recovering your account, securing it from further breaches, and reporting the hack to the platform.

  • 8. Can the cybersecurity helpline help with ransomware attacks?

    Answer: Yes, the helpline can guide you on steps to handle ransomware attacks, secure your data, and prevent further breaches.

  • 9. What should I do if I suspect someone is using my identity online?

    Answer: Contact the cybersecurity helpline to report identity theft. They can assist in mitigating the damage and securing your accounts and data.

  • 10. How can I get technical advice through the cybersecurity helpline?

    Answer: The helpline connects you with cybersecurity experts who can provide advice on securing your devices, data, and accounts against cyber threats.

  • 11. What type of awareness programs does the cybersecurity helpline support?

    Answer: The helpline promotes awareness programs on safe internet practices, recognizing cyber threats, and protecting personal information online.

  • 12. Can the helpline help with child online safety?

    Answer: Yes, the cybersecurity helpline provides guidance on protecting children from online predators, cyberbullying, and inappropriate content.

  • 13. What should I do if I am being cyberstalked or harassed?

    Answer: Report the incident to the cybersecurity helpline for assistance. They can guide you on filing a complaint and securing your online presence.

  • 14. Is there support available for senior citizens facing cyber fraud?

    Answer: Yes, the helpline provides tailored support to senior citizens, helping them report fraud and safeguard their online activities.

  • 15. Can the cybersecurity helpline assist with recovering lost data?

    Answer: While the helpline cannot directly recover data, they can guide you on steps to retrieve lost data and secure it against future loss.

  • 16. What should I do if I experience cyberbullying?

    Answer: Contact the cybersecurity helpline to report cyberbullying. They can provide guidance on taking legal steps and protecting your online interactions.

  • 17. Are the services of the cybersecurity helpline free of charge?

    Answer: Yes, the services provided by the cybersecurity helpline are free of charge.

  • 18. How can I contribute to cybersecurity awareness?

    Answer: You can contribute by reporting cyber threats, sharing safe internet practices, and volunteering in programs conducted by the helpline or related organizations.
