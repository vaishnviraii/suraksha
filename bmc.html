The BMC Helpline Services of Maharashtra is designed to offer 24-hour support for residents needing assistance with municipal services and emergency response related to issues under the jurisdiction of the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC). It provides immediate access to services such as healthcare facilities, sanitation and waste management, road maintenance, and water supply through a single, centralized helpline.

1916 is a toll-free 24-hour helpline service available for residents of Mumbai to report complaints and seek information regarding BMC services.

1916 BMC Helpline is integrated with 112 - Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) to provide a coordinated response for both municipal and emergency services as needed.

In the future, the goal is to have 112 as a single emergency number, with 1916 focusing on non-emergency municipal support and providing critical information on civic services, health programs, and other government initiatives relevant to residents.

The BMC Helpline also connects residents to various civic departments and statutory officers, such as health inspectors, environment officers, and building safety officers, ensuring that citizens receive assistance with municipal regulations and local area needs.

  • 1. What is the BMC helpline number?

    Answer: The BMC helpline number for emergencies and assistance is 1916.

  • 2. What services does the BMC helpline provide?

    Answer: The BMC helpline provides services like emergency medical assistance, waste management complaints, water supply issues, road repairs, and more.

  • 3. Is the BMC helpline available 24/7?

    Answer: Yes, the BMC helpline operates 24/7 for urgent services and emergency responses.

  • 4. Can I remain anonymous when calling the BMC helpline?

    Answer: Yes, you can remain anonymous when calling the BMC helpline. Your privacy will be respected.

  • 5. What should I do if I am facing issues with water supply?

    Answer: If you are facing water supply issues, you should immediately contact the BMC helpline at 1916 for support and resolution.

    Answer: The BMC helpline can assist by registering complaints regarding garbage collection, waste segregation issues, and other sanitation-related concerns.

  • 7. What support is available for road repair issues in Mumbai?

    Answer: The BMC helpline can help report potholes, road damages, and other infrastructure issues to the concerned authorities for prompt repair.

  • 8. Can the BMC helpline help with issues related to illegal construction?

    Answer: Yes, the BMC helpline can assist in reporting illegal construction activities and help initiate action by relevant authorities.

  • 9. What should I do if I see open manholes or unsafe areas on the roads?

    Answer: Immediately report the issue to the BMC helpline at 1916 so they can take corrective action to avoid accidents.

  • 10. How can I get information about flood-related emergency services?

    Answer: The BMC helpline provides information regarding flood-related emergency services, including evacuation, relief camps, and safe shelters during heavy rains and floods.

  • 11. What type of medical assistance can I get through the BMC helpline?

    Answer: The BMC helpline can help you access emergency medical services, ambulance services, and connect you with hospitals for immediate care.

  • 12. Can the BMC helpline help with fire and safety-related emergencies?

    Answer: Yes, the BMC helpline can connect you to fire and emergency services in case of a fire or safety hazards in your area.

  • 13. What should I do if I spot a disease outbreak or health concern in my locality?

    Answer: Report any disease outbreaks or public health concerns to the BMC helpline so that appropriate health measures can be taken by authorities.

  • 14. Is there support available for senior citizens in emergencies?

    Answer: Yes, the BMC helpline can assist senior citizens in emergencies, including medical help, relocation, or any urgent requirements they may have.

  • 15. Can the BMC helpline assist with relocating during disasters?

    Answer: Yes, the BMC helpline can assist with evacuation and provide shelter to individuals in need during natural disasters or emergency situations.

  • 16. What should I do if I notice a tree falling hazard?

    Answer: Immediately call the BMC helpline to report the hazard so that the tree can be removed or secured for safety.
