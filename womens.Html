WOMENS HELPLINE

"Instant support and safety for women in need—because your voice matters."

The Scheme of Women Helpline is intended to provide 24 hours immediate and emergency response to women affected by violence through referral (linking with appropriate authority such as police, One Stop Centre, hospital) and information about women-related government schemes programs across the country through a single uniform number.

181 is a toll-free 24-hours telecom service made available to women affected by violence seeking support and information.

181 Women Helpline would be integrated with 112 - Emergency Response Support System (ERSS) for all emergency assistance.

In future, the endeavour would be to have a single number 112 for emergency response and 181 for taking care of all non-emergency and information dissemination services to women, girls, and children.

The WHL will also provide information about government schemes/programmes, facilities, and will also connect women with the statutory officers under various legislations like Dowry Prohibition Officers, Child Marriage Prevention Officers, and Protection Officers etc. as per requirement within the local area in which she resides or is employed.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS: WOMEN'S HELPLINE

  • 1. What is the number for the women’s helpline in India?

    Answer: The national women’s helpline number in India is 181.

  • 2. What services does the women’s helpline provide?

    Answer: The women’s helpline provides services such as crisis intervention, counseling, information on legal rights, shelter assistance, medical aid, and referrals to other support services.

  • 3. Is the women’s helpline available 24/7?

    Answer: Yes, the women’s helpline operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  • 4. Can I remain anonymous when calling the women’s helpline?

    Answer: Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when calling the women’s helpline. Your privacy and confidentiality will be respected.

  • 5. What should I do if I am facing domestic violence?

    Answer: If you are facing domestic violence, you should immediately contact the women’s helpline at 181 for support and guidance. They can help you with emergency shelter, legal advice, and medical assistance.

  • 6. How can the women’s helpline assist in cases of sexual harassment at the workplace?

    Answer: The women’s helpline can provide information on your legal rights, help you file a complaint with the appropriate authorities, and offer counseling and emotional support.

  • 7. What support is available for victims of sexual assault?

    Answer: Victims of sexual assault can receive medical assistance, legal advice, counseling, and support in reporting the incident to the police. The helpline can guide you through each step of the process.

  • 8. Can the women’s helpline help with issues related to dowry harassment?

    Answer: Yes, the women’s helpline can assist with dowry harassment cases by providing legal information, support in filing complaints, and connecting you with shelter and counseling services.

  • 9. What should I do if I suspect a woman is being trafficked?

    Answer: If you suspect a woman is being trafficked, contact the women’s helpline immediately. They can coordinate with law enforcement and other agencies to provide rescue and rehabilitation services.

  • 10. How can I get legal advice through the women’s helpline?

    Answer: The women’s helpline can connect you with legal experts who can provide advice and assistance on various issues such as domestic violence, sexual harassment, divorce, and child custody.

  • 11. What type of counseling is available through the women’s helpline?

    Answer: The helpline offers emotional and psychological counseling to help women cope with trauma, stress, and other mental health issues. They can also provide referrals to professional therapists if needed.

  • 12. Can the women’s helpline help with child custody issues?

    Answer: Yes, the women’s helpline can provide information and legal assistance regarding child custody issues. They can help you understand your rights and navigate the legal process.

  • 13. What should I do if I am being stalked or harassed?

    Answer: If you are being stalked or harassed, contact the women’s helpline for support. They can provide guidance on how to stay safe, help you report the incident to the police, and offer counseling services.

  • 14. Is there support available for women with disabilities?

    Answer: Yes, the women’s helpline provides support for women with disabilities, including assistance with accessing medical care, legal aid, and other necessary services.

  • 15. Can the women’s helpline assist with relocation to a safer place?

    Answer: Yes, the women’s helpline can help arrange safe shelter and relocation for women who are in immediate danger or need to escape an abusive situation.

  • 16. What should I do if I am experiencing cyber harassment?

    Answer: If you are experiencing cyber harassment, contact the women’s helpline for guidance. They can help you report the harassment to the appropriate authorities and provide advice on how to protect yourself online.

  • 17. Are the services of the women’s helpline free of charge?

    Answer: Yes, the services provided by the women’s helpline are free of charge

  • 18. How can I volunteer or support the women’s helpline?

    Answer: To volunteer or support the women’s helpline, you can contact the helpline directly to inquire about volunteer opportunities or donate to organizations that support women’s safety and empowerment.
